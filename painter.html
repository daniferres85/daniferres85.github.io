Painter: Transform feedback using WebGL2

Camera Controls:

  • Click to Orbit around the model
  • Click (+LeftShift) to Pan the scene
  • Wheel to Zoom
  • Press 'f' to Reset the view

Painter Controls:

  • Press 'a' while clicking to Paint the model
